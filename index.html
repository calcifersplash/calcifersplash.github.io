Travel to Linarria - a place of mystery and adventure

Information

Linarria is a island just off the south coast of Mexico and is home to many different animals and birds.
It has long sandy white beaches and a history of surfing champions. Since it is located in a remote area,
Linarria had been closed to the public for many years. Now the beautiful sights are open to all!

Why you should come to Linuarria

  • It has the only naturally coloured water spring in the world.
  • Everyone is friendly and polite.
  • There are activities for all ages and ablilites.

Landmarks

One of the most famous landmarks in Linarria is The Linua Tower which has been carefully restored by the Linarrian Landmark Society in 1999 after a fire spread to the base. There are a variety of museums and art galleries, showcasing our most prized works - the most known one is probably the Mona Lisa by Leonardo DaVinci. He was inspired by the Linarrain women who worked very hard in the fields across the island so therefore invited Luia into his mansion to paint her. His mansion which he left to his good friend, Puerto, who lived on the island, is also a very popular landmark.

How to get there

Take the bus to Mexicanan Totore Lo then when you arrive, ask for a ticket to Linarria[a]. When you reach port, YOU HAVE ARRIVED! If you would like help or a tour of the island, head to the Information Center. If you can't find it, ask a friendly local. Otherwise call this universal number - 0234958464.
